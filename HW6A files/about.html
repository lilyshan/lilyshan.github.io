About

Bun Bun Bake Shop started as a family business in 1999 in Pittsburgh, PA. Since then, it has become a Pittsburgh staple for its famous cinnamon buns. While its popularity has grown, Bun Bun Bake Shop’s small bakery charm has remained the same. The shop has now expanded to six artisan flavors: Original, Blackberry, Walnut, Pumpkin Spice, and Caramel Pecan – perfect for any occassion or season. Furthermore, Bun Bun Bake Shop now has a gluten-free option, so anyone can enjoy our famous cinnamon buns!

Come stop by our shop in Oakland, or order your cinnamon buns online for pick-up!
